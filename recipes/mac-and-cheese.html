Mac & Cheese

Homepage

Description

Every child's favourite lunchtime meal that moms only allow once a month. Great just as it is but so much room for creativity.

Ingredients

  • Kraft Mac and Cheese
  • 2 Sausages
  • 100g Mozeralla Cheese Shred
  • 1 Tbsp Butter
  • 1/2 Cup Milk
  • 1 Tsp Onion Powder
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Chop your sausages into bite sized coins and add to the saucepan
  2. Follow the cooking instructions on the Kraft packaging to create your mac and cheese in the same saucepan
  3. Once your macaroni is aldente and incorporated with the packeted seasonings, add onion powder, mozerella, butter and milk to the pot
  4. Mix until all is well incorporated
  5. Enjoy while warm!
