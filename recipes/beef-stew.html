Beef Stew

Homepage

Description

Beef stew is a hearty dish suitable for lunch or dinner, slowcooked to mouth-watering perfection. In this recipe, we will be creating a big pot worth 4 servings that can be enjoyed by friends and family together.

Ingredients

  • 400g Chuck Roast Beef
  • 2 Russet Potatoes
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 3 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Tbsp Tomato Paste
  • 3 Cups Beed Stock
  • 100ml Heavy Cream
  • 1 Tbsp Corn Starch
  • 1 Bay Leaf
  • Salt and Pepper to season

Steps

  1. Cube the beef into 1-inch pieces, sear in butter until the surface is nicely browned, and set aside
  2. Chop up your vegetables and spread out on a baking try lined with baking paper
  3. Drizzle olive oil, salt and pepper and incorporate well with the vegetables
  4. Preheat oven to 200 degrees celcius, and cook vegetables for 25min, flipping them halfway
  5. Combine beef, vegetables, herbs and spices into a slow cooker, as well as the tomato paste and beef stock
  6. Set the slow cooker to slow stew
  7. After cooking, add the corn starch slurry and cream into the stew to thicken
  8. Serve warm and enjoy!
